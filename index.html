AI-Accelerated Learning Systems

A Data-Driven Framework for Cognitive Productivity and Human–AI Knowledge Collaboration

Author: Nabal Kishore Pande

ORCID: 0009-0007-3325-9966

Abstract

Artificial intelligence is transforming how humans learn and process knowledge. This paper introduces AI-accelerated learning systems and presents the NeuroGenesis Learning Framework, a structured model explaining how AI enhances learning speed, retention, and cognitive productivity through human–AI collaboration.
